Las propiedades background-clip, background-origin, background-size y el multifondo

Background-clip

background-clip: border-box (this is default): The background extends behind the border:

Behind

background-clip: padding-box: The background extends to the inside edge of the border

Inside edge

background-clip: content-box: The background extends to the edge of the content box

Edge









Background-origin

background-origin: padding-box (default):

upper left corner of the padding edge.

background-origin: border-box:

upper left corner of the border.

background-origin: content-box:

upper left corner of the content.










Background-size

background-size: auto (default):

Original size.

background-size: 300px 100px:
